Attending to Varying Shifts in Banking Habits

In 2015, the UBL Digital App and O3 Interfaces came together to redefine mobile banking apps.

Online banking wasn't particularly handy initially. Despite the fact that we could access our bank accounts from anywhere in Pakistan, there were still reservations pertaining to user-orientation. This compelled people to lean on branch banking for support and accessibility. But that didn't sit well with us. We wanted to change that.

If we had online banking, why wasn't it living up to basic standards? We had the technology already, which meant all we needed to do was tailor it to consumer needs and phone function. We succeeded in our plans because we researched from a consumer's point of view and didn't give much weight to corporate requirements.

We wanted an inclusive design for a banking app that was accommodating, convenient, and simple to use for people of all ages. Most importantly, it had to not be boring. This banking app was intended to change the face of the game. This project was challenging yet equally rewarding. We needed to appeal to the younger generation while making sure we did not compromise the older ones.

Facilitating connections and streamlining operations

After identifying the core needs and expectations of both drivers and customers, the focus shifted towards crafting an intuitive user experience complemented by robust technology solutions.

Onboarding for Driver/Fleet Owners

A streamlined and secure process to welcome drivers and fleet owners onto the platform, ensuring their identity and credentials are verified to maintain trust and safety.

Driver Availability & Request Management

Enabling drivers to signal their availability and manage requests from customers efficiently, enhancing their ability to secure jobs without the traditional wait at loading zones.

Booking and Communication

A feature-rich interface allowing for the scheduling of bookings and secure, in-app communication between customers and drivers, aiming to reduce misunderstandings and improve service satisfaction.

Tracking and Management

Tools for both customers and drivers to track bookings in real-time, offering transparency and control over the transportation process.

With a strong emphasis on simplicity and intuitiveness, we aimed at minimising cognitive load and optimising the user journey through clear visual cues, streamlined processes, and real-time notifications.
